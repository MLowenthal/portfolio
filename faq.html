FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the first step in meeting with Max?

Beginning your journey with Max Lowenthal is simple. After scheduling your first meeting, you and Max will meet up and discuss how he can be of assistance. Dont worry if you dont have an idea of what Max can do for you before meeting up, as Max can bring along some samples and help you decide which of his services are the right fit for you.

Can I only use Max for one service at once?

While Max prefers to focus on one project per customer at a time, he is willing to be flexible in terms of what he can do for you. Expect a small negotiable fee for multipule projects at once, but don't let that stop you from let Max achieve his main goal: making you into a great leader!

Does Max work alone?

Throughout his years as a leader in various organizations Max has worked with hundreds of other qualified leadership professionals. While many of these projects are primarily operated by Max, he has access to a vast network of leadership and team building experts to call upon if needed.

Are Prices Negotiable?

While most prices are non-negotiable, Max is willing to be flexible if you have financial strains or concerns. If you feel as though Max's prices are out of your price range, get in contact with him and let him know you're interested before deciding his services are too expensive.

What sets Max apart from other leadership and teamwork experts?

Max Lowenthal's drive and passion combined with his youthful point of view create a teaching expierence unlike any other. While many individuals refer to themselves as true "leadership experts", few have held leadership positions since becoming "experts". Max currently works in other leadership roles in addition to teaching others his ways, ensuring that his methods and practices are the most update and efficient.

If Max currently works with other groups, how will he have time for me?

While it is true Max currently participates in a number of leadership positons, his true passion has always helping others. It is this passion that drove him to create this service, and it is this passion that leads Max to pledge priority to any customer before himself.
