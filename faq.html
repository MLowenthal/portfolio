FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the first step in meeting with Max?

Beginning your journey with Max Lowenthal is simple. After scheduling a first time meeting in an agreed upon location, you and Max will meet up and discuss how he can be of assistance. Be sure to have an idea of what Max can do for you before meeting up, and if you dont let Max know and he can bring along some samples and help you make a decision.

Can I only use Max for one service at once?

While Max prefers to focus on one project per customer at a time, he is very flexible in terms of what he can do for you. Expect a small negotiable fee for multipule projects at once, but Max's main goal is help you become a great leader!

Does Max work alone?

Throughout his years as a leader in various organizations Max has worked with hundreds of people. While many of these projects are run only by Max, he has access to a vast network of leadership and team building experts to call upon if needed.

Are Prices Negotiable?

Well many of the prices stated on this site are final, Max is a flexible man and prefers to help any individual or group. If you feel as though Max's prices are out of your price range, get in contact with him and let him know youre interested before deciding his services are too expensive.

What sets Max apart from other leadership and teamwork experts?

Max Lowenthal's drive and passion combined with his youthful point of view combine to create a teaching expierence unlike any other. While many people refer to themselves as true "leadership experts", few have been in positions of leadership in many years. Max currently works in other leadership roles in addition to teaching others his ways, ensuring that his methods and practices are the most update and efficient.

If Max currently works with other groups, how will he have time for me?

While it is true Max currently participates in a number of leadership positons, his true passion has always helping others. It is this passion that drove him to create this service in the first place and it is this passion that leads Max to pledge priority to any customer before himself.
