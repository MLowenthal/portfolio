Do you learn better in a one-on-one setting? Have a face to face meeting with Max yourself. Small Group Workshops

Do you regularly work in a small team setting? Bring in Max to meet with your team and discuss a variety of topics. From finding each team members' special skills, to the role that each member plays in the team, Max's skills are applicable to any size group, and you're sure to see an increase in productivity and worker happiness after just one session with Max. Many groups also chose to bring back Max for a few additional follow up sessions, ensuring that Max's methods produce real results and allowing him to correct any issues your team might be having.
