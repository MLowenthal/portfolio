A History of Leadership

Max Lowenthal has a vast and diverse history of leadership, however he his humble beginnings in his religious youth group and private high school are what he truly credits as the beginning of his leadership journey.

It all began when he attended his first United Synagouge Youth (USY) Convention in the Fall of 2010. As a new member to the organization, Max was impressed by the attendence and diversity of the event, but knew that the organization was capable of so much more. Max went on to climb the levels of USY's leadership hierachy, starting at the local level in his own hometown of St. Paul, MN. He eventually reached an International Level position in Online Services for the organiztion.

In addition to his work with USY throughout high school, Max was also very active in planning social events and assemblies for his high school. Beginning with a Simple 10th Grade Representative postion in his school's Student Activties Commitee, Max once again saw potential for the organization to increase student moral and overall happiness, and took it into his own hands to make it so. By the time he had graduated, Max had become a Co-President of the Organization, and planned a number of school wide events that many students and faculty called the best they had ever seen.
